Who We Are...

Established in 2006, Shraddha Shilai Machine has been a trusted name in sewing machines, motors, and spare parts for both household and industrial use. With nearly two decades of experience, we ensure high-performance, durable, and affordable sewing solutions for our customers.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us build long-lasting relationships and a loyal client base across the region. We offer a wide selection of both traditional and modern sewing machines, backed by expert guidance, after-sales support, and timely servicing. Whether you're a beginner, a professional tailor, or managing a large-scale textile unit, Shraddha Shilai Machine is your one-stop destination for all your sewing needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable sewing machines that empower individuals and businesses in the tailoring and fashion industry. We are committed to delivering excellent customer service, technical support, and innovative sewing solutions to enhance creativity and craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading sewing machine provider known for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We aim to support small businesses, entrepreneurs, and hobbyists by offering the best sewing technology and services that inspire creativity and growth.
